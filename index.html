01 — About Me

Who I Am

I am Rowdra Dip Chackroborty, an Urban & Regional Planning graduate from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), currently working as an Assistant Urban Planner at Sheltech Consultants Pvt. Ltd., Dhaka.

My expertise lies at the intersection of GIS & remote sensing, sustainable urban development, and environmental planning — with a strong focus on water resource management, flood risk assessment, and climate-resilient community planning.

As a researcher, I have co-authored publications in Springer Nature and international conferences, and currently have 5 research papers under review or in progress. I am passionate about translating spatial data into actionable planning decisions for a sustainable future.

Languages: Bengali (native) · English (C1/C2 — Proficient)
